Choosing a path in life is difficult

We are here to help you imagine the many different pathways that are available to you after college

We hope to help undergraduates at UC Berkeley see what paths are available to different college majors to help assist in making a decision as to what major is right for them. The goal is to answer questions like: What graduate programs do people in my major go into? What types of industries do they end up in?



What is valuable to users

Providing the right metrics for major exploration

We surveyed UC Berkeley undergraduates to get a sense of what metrics were most important to them so that we could create visualization that could help them explore different majors and their career paths in a meaningful way.





The process

Using data from UC Berkey's Career Center, we processed and transformed them into meaningful data visualizations

We noted specific trends that are present in the data and tried to illustrate the jobs that provided the most flexibility as well as majors that provided the most flexibility as well as allowing users to explore the data by themselves through choosing and sorting the metrics that are the most important to them.
